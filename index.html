About Kira

Meet Our Little Champion ❤️

⭐ 🏑 ⭐

Hi everyone! My name is Kira du Plessis — I'm 11 years old, I play Link (#4) for Laerskool Midvaal's U/11 A-Team, and hockey is absolutely everything to me.

Last year, at just 10 years old, I was already playing U/11 A-team hockey. In 2025, I scored the winning goal that made us the U/11 Sedibeng East League Champions — honestly the best moment of my life. That goal taught me what it feels like to give everything and win.

In 2026 I've been selected again for the U/11 A-team. I've also been selected to tour to Jubaweni (19–21 June) — to learn the skills and lessons to succeed, and face the challenges that lie ahead.

When we come back from tour, our season starts — and we're going back to fight for that title again and make Laerskool Midvaal proud. But I also have a bigger dream: I'm working hard this year to earn selection for the D7 squad, which would give me the chance to play in Gauteng in 2027. Step by step, I'm getting there! 🏑💛

From the bottom of my heart — thank you for believing in me!

— Kira du Plessis  ❤️  #4 The Link  ⭐ U/11 A-Team

Kira's Hockey Journey

From Winning Goal to Gauteng ⭐

Three chapters of one incredible story — and the best is still ahead.

🏆
2025

League Champions & The Winning Goal

At just 10 years old, Kira was already competing at A-team level. In a defining league match, she stepped up and scored the winning goal — crowning Laerskool Midvaal the U/11 Sedibeng East League Champions 2025. The whole team erupted. That moment changed her life forever.

⚽ Winning Goal 🏆 League Champions 👧 Age 10

2026

Selected Again — Tour to Jubaweni

Kira has been selected again for the U/11 A-team and earned her tour selection to Jubaweni (19–21 June 2026). The tour is about more than just competing — it's about learning the skills, facing the challenges, and building the resilience that champions are made of. When she returns, the season begins — and the title defence starts. Every rand you donate puts her on that bus.

2027 — The Dream

D7 Squad Selection → Play in Gauteng

Kira's eyes are already on 2027. Her goal is to earn selection for the D7 squad — a highly competitive district level that opens the door to playing in Gauteng. That would be the biggest step yet: competing against the best young hockey players in the province. Every match she plays in 2026 is a stepping stone to that goal. Your support helps build that future.
