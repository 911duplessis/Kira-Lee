About Kira

Her Story In Her Words ❤️

⭐ 🏑 ⭐

Hi everyone! My name is Kira-Lee du Plessis, I'm 11 years old, and I just got the biggest news of my life: I've been selected for the Athletics Vaal Triangle team to compete in the SA 1 Mile Championships in Gqeberha!

I run for Nedbank RC in the 11–13 Girls category, and this race is part of the SA 21.1km, 5km & 1 Mile Championships on 27 June 2026 — incorporated into the Nelson Mandela Bay Half Marathon. Athletes from clubs all over the Vaal Triangle were selected on qualifying times, and I made the team!

Before this, hockey was my whole world — at just 10 years old I scored the winning goal that made Laerskool Midvaal the U/11 Sedibeng East League Champions in 2025, and last week our community came together and fully funded my hockey tour to Jubaweni. Now a whole new dream has opened up: representing my province on the track.

My family doesn't have much money right now, but my dad always tells me that with faith, hope and love, anything is possible. I'm working so hard for this — and one day, I'd love to try pole vault too. Thank you for being part of my journey! 🇿🇦🏃💛

— Kira-Lee du Plessis  |  AVT 1 Mile Team  |  Nedbank RC
