About Kira

Meet Our Little Champion ❤️

⭐ 🏑 ⭐

Hi everyone! My name is Kira du Plessis — I'm 11 years old, I play Link (#4) for Laerskool Midvaal's A-Team U/11, and hockey is everything to me.

Last year, at just 10 years old, I was already playing A-team hockey. In 2025 I scored the winning goal that made us Sedibeng-Oos Liga Champions — honestly the best moment of my life. That goal taught me what it feels like to give everything and win.

In 2026 I've been selected again for the A-team tour to Jubaweni (19–21 June). We're going back to fight for that title and make Laerskool Midvaal proud. But I also have a bigger dream: I'm working hard this year to earn selection for the D7 squad, which would give me the chance to play in Gauteng in 2027. Step by step, I'm getting there! 🏑💛

I'm passionate. I'm fast and agile. I'm not scared of anything. Every donation, no matter how small, brings me one step closer to that Gauteng field. Thank you for believing in me!

— Kira du Plessis  ❤️  #4 The Link  ⭐ A-Team U/11

Kira's Hockey Journey

From Winning Goal to Gauteng ⭐

Three chapters of one incredible story — and the best is still ahead.

🏆
2025

Liga Champions & The Winning Goal

At just 10 years old, Kira earned her place on the A-Team U/11 — the youngest and most competitive level. In a deciding league match, she stepped up and scored the winning goal that crowned Laerskool Midvaal the Sedibeng-Oos Liga Champions 2025. The whole team erupted. That goal changed everything.

⚽ Winning Goal 🏆 Liga Champions 👧 Age 10
🏑
2026

Selected Again — Tour to Jubaweni

Kira has been selected for the A-Team U/11 again in 2026, earning her place as #4 — The Link. The Link position demands speed, vision, and the ability to both defend and attack — and Kira delivers all three. The team heads to Jubaweni (19–21 June 2026) to defend their reputation and chase another title. This is the tour we need your help to fund.

🏑 #4 The Link 📍 Jubaweni Tour 💰 R3 350 Needed
🌟
2027 — The Dream

D7 Squad Selection → Play in Gauteng

Kira's eyes are already on 2027. Her goal is to earn selection for the D7 squad — a highly competitive district level that opens the door to playing in Gauteng. That would be the biggest step yet: competing against the best young hockey players in the province. Every match she plays in 2026 is a stepping stone to that goal. Your support helps build that future.
